Folklorico


Folklorico is where you dance traditional cultural dances that emphasize local folk culture with ballet characteristics -- pointed toes, exaggerated movements and highly choreographed. The purpose why I am showing this is to entertain and to teach. I have been doing folklorico for two years going on my third year. I found out about folklorico when I was in middle school when the folklorico high school group came to perform. I fell in love when they started dancing. The movements, the heels, the dress, it is all so beautiful and pretty. Especially the dances and the history behind the dance. I selected this sample to share because it's a part of my culture and it takes so much work to do it because dancing folklorico isn't easy at all, especially with trying to keep balance and while holding a heavy dress at the level where it's supposed to maintain and remembering the counts.
