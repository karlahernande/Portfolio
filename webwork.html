DelveHomes


We were assigned to create a website out of this story we read in English Class. The story we read is called "The Machine Stops". It's about how humans have lost the ability to live on the surface of earth. They now live in these underground homes, each person has their own room. There is this machine (robot) who brings them food and brings their clothes. They even have buttons to press in need of assistance.


After we read that story in English, everyone got paird with someone of their choice. The task was to create a website of these homes and try to sell them to potential buyers looking for underground homes. Me and my partner first started off by creating our own individual mood boards, we used canva during this process. When using canva I learned how to create color palettes. I created my first 3 and he created his 2. Then from there we ended up picking one of my mood boards. From there we used Figma and started planning out our wireframes on how they were going to look for our website. The goal of using Figma is using it to make our wireframes and prototypes. We had some challenges at first with the Figma since it was out first wireframe and we were learning how to use the tools.


After we successfully finished our wireframes, we started coding on our website using Visual Studio Code (VS Code). The goal of using vs code is to code for our website and get the pages connected and images connected to take you to another page of the website. The goal was to also write our HTML and CSS because those two are important. Me and my partner collaborted by him doing the CSS and me doing the HTML. When he finished the CSS and when I finished the HTML we combined them together. We communicated a lot during this process to clarify if it is how we want it to look like. When we finished we used Netlify to publish our website.
